Aria's Expertise !

Posted on December 21, 2023 by Aria McComber

Embarking on a career in supply chain sustainability, I envision a path that merges my academic expertise with real-world impact. My journey in this field began with a profound interest in environmental conservation and a commitment to addressing the challenges posed by electronic waste (e-waste). As a graduate student specializing in supply chain sustainability, my primary focus has been on understanding the intricacies of e-waste management within the corporate landscape.

My individual experience in this field includes hands-on research and internships that have provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities within the electronic industry. My research is focused on discovering country characteristics that impact e-waste collection rates. Feel free to reach out for a more in-depth explanation. A pass internship I would like to highlight was my time at BanQu. BanQu is a blockchain software company that increases supply chain transparency. Through these experiences, I've gained insights into the current practices of electronic waste disposal and recycling, identifying areas for improvement and innovation.

In my projected career path, I aim to contribute to the electronic industry's sustainable transformation by implementing efficient and environmentally friendly supply chain practices. I aspire to work closely with corporations to develop strategies that minimize e-waste generation, optimize recycling processes, and promote the adoption of circular economy principles. By doing so, I hope to play a pivotal role in reducing the environmental footprint of electronic products and fostering a more sustainable future.

My anticipated contributions to this field:

Anticipating my contributions to the field, I am committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry. Through ongoing research and collaboration with key stakeholders, I aim to develop practical solutions that can be implemented on a global scale. My work is not just about addressing immediate concerns but fostering a long-term shift towards a circular economy, where the life cycle of electronic products is designed for minimal environmental impact.

In joining the electronic field with a focus on supply chain sustainability, my goal is to be a catalyst for positive change. By influencing corporate practices, I aspire to contribute to a world where electronic products are not only technologically advanced but also ethically and sustainably produced, ultimately benefitting both the industry and the planet.
